### Professional Summary

Frontend developer with over twenty years of experience building web applications and single-page applications for automotive, pharmaceutical, entertainment, and advertising industries. Specializes in modern development workflows, TypeScript, and scalable monorepo architectures. Currently leveraging AI tools to maximize development efficiency and code quality.

I excel in diverse environments—from solo development to collaborative teams using Trello, GitHub, and Slack. Strong believer that **communication is the key** to successful projects.

**Personal Mission:** Maximize efficiency and leverage AI as much as possible in the development process.
